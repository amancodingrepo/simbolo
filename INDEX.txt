================================================================================
                     ANIMATION REQUIREMENTS
================================================================================

Scroll-triggered animations needed:
- effectFade (base class)
- fadeUp, fadeDown, fadeLeft, fadeRight (directional)
- fadeZoom (zoom variant)
- fadeRotateX, fadeRotateY (3D rotation)
- img-transform-3 (applied to images)

Triggered by: Intersection Observer (data-inviewport="yes")

Recommended library: Framer Motion OR AOS OR custom hooks

Counter animations:
- Property: data-speed (duration in ms)
- Property: data-to (target number)
- Property: data-inviewport="yes" (trigger on view)
- Recommended library: react-countup

Carousel animations:
- Property: data-clone="5" (infinite loop clones)
- Recommended library: Swiper JS
